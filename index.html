Some random information about pizza.

Pizza is considered the most popular food in the world as a consequence of the Italian exodus that settled in America throughout the 20th century.

Since 1989, the World Pizza Championship has been held annually in Italy, holding various competitions and rewarding those who obtain the first three places in each of them.

The antecedents of pizza are found in the use of wheat bread in the ancient cultures of Egypt, Persia, Greece and Rome.

Pizza in its most traditional versions, marinara and covered in tomato sauce and mozzarella (Neapolitan), comes from the Italian city of Naples.

The lazzarone usually eats only two things: pizza and cocomero;... cocomero in summer, pizzas in winter... Pizza is a kind of talmouse as it is made in Saint-Denis; it is round in shape and is kneaded with the same dough as bread. It comes in different widths, depending on the price. A two liards pizza is enough for a man; a two sous pizza should fill the whole family. At first glance, pizza seems like a simple dish; upon examination, it is a mixed plate.
